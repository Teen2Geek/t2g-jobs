About Us

Teen2Geek is an initiative to create a better teen development community by

  • providing educational resources to expand knowledge
  • facilitating a social news platform for teen developers similar to HN
  • curating a internship board targeted towards teens

T2G Jobs is a place for teen developers to find local and remote internships to participate in, thus furthering their experience. It is heavily curated, so each and every internship submission is reviewed for quality and legitimacy by a moderator from Teen2Geek.
